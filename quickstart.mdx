## Launch a token

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    Go to [app.pumpevm.fun](https://app.pumpevm.fun) and connect.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the token">
    Click **Launch Token** and provide name, symbol, description, and logo. it's Free!!!
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pre-Bond phase">
    Buy tokens with ETH. Purchased tokens are locked until the curve is finalized.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bonding phase">
    Buy and sell freely. Sell fees are dynamic (0.1% – 2%) based on 24h volume.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finalize to Uniswap V3">
    When real ETH reaches 6.9, the creator can call `finalizeCurve()` to migrate liquidity. Pre-bond tokens become transferable.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Trade perpetuals

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect at trade.pumpevm.fun">
    Visit [trade.pumpevm.fun](https://trade.pumpevm.fun).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deposit collateral">
    USDC, USDT
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open a position">
    Choose market, leverage (up to 50x), and place a long or short.
  </Step>
</Steps>
