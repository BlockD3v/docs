## Token creator program

- 95% of launchpad fees from tokens you create go to you.
- Featured token launches and marketing support.
- Transparent leaderboards.

## Top holders program

- 1% of all fees distributed to the top 5 holders.
- Monthly distribution based on holdings.

## Prediction market builder program

- 50% of prediction market fees go to market makers.
- Earn USDC by providing liquidity.
- Create your own prediction markets.
- Referral rewards.

## Developer program

- Comprehensive documentation.
- Sepolia testnet (migration will happen on Base Mainnet) environment.
- Bug bounty program.
- Integration support.
