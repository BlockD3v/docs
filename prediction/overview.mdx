The prediction markets product enables onchain event forecasting with a high-performance CLOB and UMA oracle settlement.

## Key features

- Event creation and trading.
- Market-based forecasting.
- Leaderboards and competitions.
- UMA oracle integration for settlement.
- Instant settlement after event resolution.
- Low fees: **1%** trading fee, **0.5%** settlement fee.

## Fees

| Fee Type | Amount | Recipient |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Trading | 1% | 50% market makers(recoup), 50% platform |
| Settlement | 0.5% | Platform |
