Market makers provide liquidity to prediction markets and earn **puUSD** — a stablecoin pegged 1:1 to USD.

- 50% of all trading fees are distributed to market makers.
- puUSD can be redeemed 1:1 for USDC or USDT at any time.
- puUSD is also accepted as collateral for perpetuals.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deposit puUSD">
    Mint or transfer puUSD into your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide liquidity">
    Allocate puUSD to one or more prediction markets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn 50% of fees">
    Fees accrue automatically and can be claimed at any time.
  </Step>
</Steps>
