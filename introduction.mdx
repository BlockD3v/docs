**pumpEVM.fun** is a multi-chain onchain economy platform that brings together token launches, spot & perpetual trading, and prediction markets into one unified interface. Built for the EVM ecosystem. Social by design. Built for discovery and competition.

## Mission

Trade tokens, perps, and prediction markets — all from one unified interface. Launch memes, trade perps, and predict the future.
